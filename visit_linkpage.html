Planning Your Visit to the National Shrine of The Divine Mercy

Thank you for choosing us as your Pilgrimage Destination!

We hope that this information will help you to plan your visit in a manner that allows you to relax and enjoy your visit to the Shrine. We look forward to meeting you when you are here!

Families and friends in groups of less than 15 do NOT need to register to come to the Shrine! Please feel free to come join us!

Groups of 15 or more are asked to register with us,
as our facilities and staff are limited:

  • * Registrations for pilgrimages are now open for 2022,
    with the exception of a few special events:
  • * Registrations for our Filipino Day on June 25 are now open
    for groups of 15 or more!
  • * Registrations for Encuentro Latino on August 6 will open the week of May 23-27. Please check back here for more information.
  • * We are working to plan a Vietnamese Day on August 27, and hope to open registrations soon.
  • * Registrations will open soon for Polish Day on September 17.

Please note: Whether you come by car or bus, if a priest or deacon will be accompanying you and wish to concelebrate with us, please read the section linked here, as the paperwork CAN take up to three weeks to obtain, and we are to receive it a month in advance.
