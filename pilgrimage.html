What is a Pilgrimage?

Like our journey here, on Earth, a pilgrimage is a journey of going away from our home to another place with the purpose of encountering God in a new way.

A pilgrimage to our Shrine is an opportunity to be with our Lord, experienced in:

  • The natural beauty of Eden Hill
  • The Shrine Church
  • The outdoor shrines
  • The daily services, including Mass, Confessions, and our daily devotions.
  • The Way of The Cross, the Grotto of Lourdes, the candle shrines and Oratory Chapel.

Our gift shop will allow you to take home a piece of Eden Hill, to help you to continue to grow in your faith, even after you leave.

We hope that your time spent here will deepen your relationship with our Lord; and provide a place of refuge, to which we hope you will want to return as you continue on your spiritual journey.
