Stations of the Cross

Life-size statues take you on a unique pilgrimage …

The Stations of the Cross are outside, and therefore able to be visited by Pilgrims at any time!

The Shrine’s life-size Stations of the Cross are featured in a beautiful prayer book from Marian Press available at DivineMercyGiftShop.org.

These unique, life-size Stations of the Cross have been painstakingly sketched, sculpted, and cast. After a journey from concept to reality, they now stand on the grounds of the National Shrine of The Divine Mercy.
